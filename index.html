Our purpose and goals

The Omihachiman Chamber of Commerce was organized with the intent to promote the city and the local business by presenting the places you can visit in Omihachiman, the history of the city, its importance to Shiga province and, of course, the local business of our city.
Our goal is to increase the number of visitors to our city and consequently promote the local business increasing their profits and attract more investors and new business to our city.
